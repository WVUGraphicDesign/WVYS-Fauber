Congratulations to Armaan Karimpour, who has been selected as the winner of the 2020 WVYS Concerto Competition, and to runner-up, Emma Maconaughey!

All contestants have made their conductor, teachers, and WVYS very proud with their preparedness, professional demeanor, and evident passion for music and performing. The adjudicators were very impressed with the incredible talent and dedication our students presented on Saturday and look forward to hearing about more of their future accomplishments. GREAT JOB, EVERYBODY!
